PRODUCTS

1.BUSINESS CARDS

We print business cards of all shapes and sizes. Be it single sided business cards or double sided cards, one colour or full colour. We can handle the job for you.

2.LETTERHEADS

We will print your company letterheads. Letterheads are usually A4 in size and can be printed in black and white or in full colour. We can use 80 gram paper that is printer friendly, or any other papers of your choice or even on recycled paper.

3.FOLDERS

We print presentation folders which are very useful for presenting documents to clients in a professional and neat way. Presentation folders are usually sized to fit standard A4 size paper. Standard folders are printed with your company logo on the front and contact details on the back. Folders have an inner sleeve that is used to keep the documents in place, as well as two slits to slip in a business card.

4.BANNERS

Nippy Print is able to print PVC Banners, Vinyl banners, Canvas banners and other media. The size is up to 1300mm wide by almost any height. We print outdoor banners, pull-up banners, teardrop banners, in-store banners as well as magnetic branding for cars. Pull-Up Banners come with a stand and a bag, so they are easy to transport and pull up anywhere. They are very easy to assemble and are lightweight. Pull-Up Banners are great for travellers and for mobile corporate functions and can be re-used many times.

5.FLYERS

We print flyers of all shapes and sizes. Single sided flyers or double sided, one colour or full colour flyers. We print flyers of all shapes and sizes. Single sided flyers or double sided, one colour or full colour flyers. We can handle the job for you.We can handle the job for you.

6.BROCHURES

We print brochures of all shapes and sizes. Booklets can be folded or stapled. Brochures are usually printed on 150 – 300 gram paper and can be black and white or full colour brochures. The formats of brochures can vary but the most common are A5 brochures, A4 brochures and DL size brochures. They can be folded from A3 to A4, from A4 to A5 and also a tri-fold from A4 – DL. We can also handle other custom sizes of brochure printing, so please feel free to contact us or send us your artwork so we can advise you.

7.STICKERS

We can print matt self adhesive stickers for example vitamin bottles. Or on four year heavy duty vinyl to almost any size that you would like.Our advanced machines can also kiss cut to any shape predesigned with an outline of any shape imaginable. This being die cut lettering or company logo's trimmed out of the vinyl for window or vehicle branded application. Your sticker can be printed in full colour or black and white.

8.LABELS

We also print adhesive labels on paper or on vinyl and to almost any size that you would like. Our advanced machines can also kiss cut them so that you can peel off parts leaving behind only the part of the label that you want in place. Your label can be printed in full colour or black and white.

9.INVITATIONS

Invitations can be printed in most shapes and sizes.Invitations can be printed single sided or double sided in black and white or in full colour. The most common sizes are A4 invitations, A5 invitations, A6 invitations and DL size invitations(9.9cm X 21cm), however we can print most custom size invitations as well. Please feel free to contact us or send us your artwork so we can advise you.
